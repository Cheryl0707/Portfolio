Acoustic Chapel Bluetooth Speaker

We were inspired by the Notre Dame de Paris and the Victor Hugo novel of the same name to create a bluetooth speaker with a distinctive gothic style modeled after the cathedral. We incorporated the pointed arches, stained-glasses windows, ribbed vaults, and spires into the speaker form and designed the speaker to provide an immersive acoustic experience and excellent aesthetic experience for the user. The main Bluetooth speaker is stored in the middle under the engraved wood panel and users can remotely control the speaker with digital devices.

Intro

This project is inspired by Notre Dame de Paris and the novel's plot with t he same name as the architecture. The architecture is represented with a distinctive gothic style.

We love the pointed arches, stained-glasses windows, ribbed vaults and spires. The main character of Notre Dame de Paris novel, Quasimodo, is the bell ringer of Notre Dame de Paris. The bell ringing brings the user to the cathedral of Notre Dame in 1482, according to Victor Hugo. And our carrier is sound, we hope to provide an abundant users experience visually and auditorily.
