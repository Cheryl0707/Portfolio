Combined Elements

Chapel Speaker

We were inspired by the Notre Dame de Paris and the Victor Hugo novel of the same name to create a bluetooth speaker with a distinctive gothic style modeled after the cathedral. We incorporated the pointed arches, stained-glasses windows, ribbed vaults, and spires into the speaker form and designed the speaker to provide an immersive acoustic experience and excellent aesthetic experience for the user.

Music Recitals in Holland Park

The musical recital is a relax and chill open space in Holland Park. We sought to integrate nature into our design ethos. Our emphasis on nature led us to strategically position the pavilion to maximize natural light penetration, creating an interior space that connects intimately with the sun's radiance throughout the day. This deliberate choice enhances the visitors' immersion in the natural surroundings upon entering the pavilion.
